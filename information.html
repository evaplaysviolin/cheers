Information

We will be married in a private ceremony on Wednesday, September 27th, 2017.

The wedding reception will take place the following Saturday afternoon, on September 30th, 2017, at the Lake Merced Boathouse in San Francisco. The address is One Harding Road, San Francisco, CA 94132.

Questions

Please click on the questions below to view the answers.

What time is the reception?

The reception will begin at 12:00pm, and will end around 3:00pm.

How do I get to the reception venue?

Click me for Google directions!
Please note that the actual boathouse is located a little further in than the specified address, and will be on the right-hand side as you drive through.

Will there be parking?

Yes, and parking is free. Because Lake Merced is a public space, the parking lots will not be exclusive to the reception guests; there should still be plenty of spots to go around. There will be parking spaces along the road leading up to the boathouse as well as directly in front, and an "unofficial" dirt lot just past the building.

Is the boathouse wheelchair accessible?

Yes. The parking lot, reception room, and the patio overlooking the lake are all on the same level.

Why are you only holding a reception, and not including the ceremony?

We would like the ceremony to be a small affair, with only our immediate family members present. You won't be missing anything, we promise!

What attire is recommended?

There is no official dress code. We (the bride and groom) will be leaning more towards the formal end of the spectrum, but please feel free to dress casually and comfortably!

Will there be food?

Yes! There will be a buffet-style lunch, with appetizers before and dessert after.

Will there be alcohol?

There will be beer and wine available for those who wish to partake.

What are the accommodation options for out-of-town guests?

We currently do not have a room block set up at any hotel. Lake Merced is located in the southwest corner of the city, near Daly City. A suggestion would be to check out Airbnb's in the area. Please feel free to reach out to us if you need any help finding a place to stay!
